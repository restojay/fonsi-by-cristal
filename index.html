Meet Your Stylist

Cristal Gutierrez

Cristal is an artist when it comes to her craft with over twelve years experience. Her education includes several advanced haircutting, color and makeup classes.

As a Freelance Makeup Artist and Texas Licensed Cosmetologist with over ten years experience in salon and studio for runway, bridal, headshots, pageants and special events, Cristal brings artistry and precision to every client.

Currently the owner and operator of a full service salon in San Antonio, Texas, offering client makeup applications for all occasions with experience in fashion styling and on-set styling, plus full hair services for women and men.

Texas Licensed MAC Pro Certified Olaplex Pro 12+ Years Bridal Specialist
